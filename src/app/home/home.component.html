Frequently Asked Question

FAQs for Drivers

  • Be at least 19 years of age (may vary by province).
  • Possess a valid Canadian driver's license.
  • Have a clean driving record (within a recent time frame, like the past 3 years).
  • Own a vehicle that meets safety standards and has at least four seats.
  • Pass a criminal background check.
  • Download the TogetherVgo app.
  • Create an account as a driver.
  • Provide your personal information, driver's license details, and vehicle information.
  • Consent to a background check.
  • Wait for your profile to be approved.

FAQs for Riders

  • Download the TogetherVgo app.
  • Create an account as a rider.
  • Search for rides by entering your starting point, destination, and desired date/time.
  • Filter results by price, driver rating, etc.
  • Select the ride that best suits your needs.
  • Request a seat and wait for the driver's approval.
  • Pay securely through the app.
